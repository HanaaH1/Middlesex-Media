Pokemon Card Vendor Robbed at Gunpoint During Card Event

WOODBRIDGE, Middlesex County, NJ — A 27-year-old man and his 69-year-old father attended a Pokemon Card Collector's event and were robbed of $150,000 worth of cards.

At around 4:30 PM on June 28, they were packing up their cards in the parking lot of the APA Hotel when two men in a white BMW X3 approached them.

The suspects demanded the cards at gunpoint and escaped with $12,000 in cash and the full collection.

The case is still under investigation by the Woodbridge Police Department.
