Pokemon Card Vendor Robbed at Gunpoint During Card Event

WOODBRIDGE, Middlesex County, NJ —A 27-year-old man and his 69-year old father attended a Pokemon Card Collector's event, and were robbed $150k worth of cards.

At around 4:30pm on June 28, they were packing up their cards in the parking lot of the APA Hotel. Suddenly, two men in a white BMW X3 demanded the cards and pointed a gun toward both of them.

It turns out that the thieves escaped with $12,000 in cash from a green crossbody bag alongisde the $150,000 worth of Pokemon cards. These were hidden in a gray suitcase as they were seen escaping.

This remains under investigation by the Woodbridge Police Department. Feel free to contact them with any information at 732-634-7700.
