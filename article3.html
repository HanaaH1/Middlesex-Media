All You Need to Know In Order to Vote in New jersey!

Middlesex, Somerset, and Union County, New Jersey—

Voting is one of the most important and most valuable rights we have as Americans. Read below to find some early voting locations in the following counties.

Middlesex County: J.F.K Public Library Community Arts Center Acacia Youth Center New Brunswick Hub Teen Center

Somerset County: North Plainfield Community Center Bridgewater Township Municipal Court Bernards Township Community Center

Union County: Berkeley Heights Municipal Building Rahway Recreation Center Westfield Memorial Library Sampson Center

Remember that if you want to vote on Election Day, you must go to your assigned voting location. Find your location and get your voting questions answered at www.nj.gov/state/elections
